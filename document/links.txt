cv2.TM_CCOEFF_NORMED: Normalized cross-correlation, used to find the strongest linear relationship between the template and the search area.
cv2.TM_SQDIFF: Sum of squared differences, identifies regions with the smallest differences.
cv2.TM_CCORR: Cross-correlation, which identifies regions with similar patterns.
